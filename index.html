⬆ Consider higher protein if:
· Disease-specific needs (renal / burns / cardiac)
· Additional therapies — RRT
· Faltering growth neonates (up to 4 g/kg)
· Large wounds or other losses
· Other co-morbidities (e.g. chylothorax)
